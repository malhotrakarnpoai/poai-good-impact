## Problem Statements (Challenges)

**Main Challenge Theme**: "Help Goa Become a Sustainable Paradise"
**Context**: How can we use AI to encourage conscious consumerism among tourists and locals to reduce waste (especially during busy seasons) and create year-round, sustainable solutions?

### Challenge 1A: Fashion Waste - Circular Design (Pre-Production & Concept Stage)
- **Focus**: Circular design of products in fashion and lifestyle industry
- **Stage**: Design and concept stage
- **Tech's Role**: Help ensure designs and cuts are low waste and use the right materials
- **Outcome**: Materials created on the path of being 'lower waste' and 'circular'

### Challenge 1B: Fashion Waste - Post-Consumer Waste Management & Segregation
- **Focus**: Post-consumer waste management
- **Tech's Role**: Technology solutions for segregating items/products for easy redirection into reuse or repurposing
- **Current State**: Most fashion waste ends up in landfills

### Challenge 2A: Sustainable Habits - Digital Cues for Everyday Living
- **Focus**: Encouraging everyday sustainable habits through digital cues
- **Stage**: During daily routine planning and movement
- **The Item**: A personal digital reminder experience
- **Tech's Role**: Explore gentle, context-aware prompts that support users in remembering sustainable choices as they go about their day
- **Outcome**: Users naturally build mindful habits through subtle nudges and reflection

### Challenge 2B: Conscious Consumption - Long-term Engagement & Progress Tracking
- **Focus**: Building long-term engagement with conscious consumption
- **Stage**: After actions or purchases across a period of time
- **The Item**: A personal progress and reflection experience
- **Tech's Role**: Use simple data inputs or digital tracking to help individuals notice patterns in their sustainable practices and celebrate mindful behaviour
- **Outcome**: A supportive, engaging system that encourages ongoing commitment to conscious living without pressure or judgement
